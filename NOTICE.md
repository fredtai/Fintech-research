### FRED API
- **Source**: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/
- **Provider**: Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis
- **Usage**: US macroeconomic data, interest rates, FX rates
- **Free basis**: Free official API provided by the Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis
  - Documentation: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/docs/api/fred/
  - Rate limit: 120 requests per minute

### HKEX News
- **Source**: https://www1.hkexnews.hk/
- **Provider**: Hong Kong Exchanges and Clearing Limited (HKEX)
- **Usage**: HKEX-listed company announcements, regulatory filings, circulars
- **Free basis**: Public disclosure platform operated by the exchange; freely accessible

### NewsAPI
- **Source**: https://newsapi.org/
- **License**: Proprietary (free tier available)
- **Usage**: Global financial news headlines
- **Free basis**: Free tier includes 100 requests per day
  - Developer plan: https://newsapi.org/pricing

### RSS Feeds
- **Source**: Various public financial RSS feeds
- **Usage**: Supplementary news aggregation
- **Free basis**: RSS is an open web standard; feeds are publicly accessible

### SEC EDGAR
- **Source**: https://www.sec.gov/edgar
- **Provider**: U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission
- **Usage**: US company filings (10-K, 10-Q, 8-K, etc.)
- **Free basis**: Free official API provided by the SEC
  - Documentation: https://www.sec.gov/edgar/sec-api-documentation
